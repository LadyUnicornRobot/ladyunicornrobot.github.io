Rule of Proximity

How Many Plants

howmanyplants.com

In this image there are six horizontal blocks of art and type. Theplants and other things are in close proximity as well as the blocks which means that even though the text is spread out, it still reads as one phrase.

Fitt's Law

Coolors

coolors.com

In this image there are only three items to click and the one to "Start the generator!" is large, boldly colored, and set in a place that is ease to touch on a phone screen.

Repetition

Jessica Hische

jessicahische.is

I love Jessica Hisch's website because of all the wonderful details she's added. This website represents repetition in the colors and in the way she uses yellow as a divider, drop cap, and squiggle underlines that identify a clickable link.
