Color Scheme and Usage:

Main color: dark blue
Used for header and footer background color, nav button background and/or text color.

Contrast color: yellow
Used as a border to highlight clickable icons such as social media icons and the map. Used to highlight important content in the page such as the daily weather report or weekly weather at-a-glance.

Secondary contrast color: light blue
Used as nav bar background color. Used as border to emphasize news article. Possibly this or white used as text color on a dark blue button.

Basic colors: black and white
Use white as text color on header and footer. White as background color of site body. Black as body copy text color.
