Target Audience

Idaho locals or visitors who are looking for something fun to do.

Two Personas

Persona #1: Active Mom on Vacation

She drives a minivan, has a child-carrier/diaper bag hiking backpack. Her and her older kids wear baseball caps from a sporting goods store. She wants to have a fun challenge in a place where she can keep track of her kids. She wears tank tops, likes rock climbing when she can find a babysitter. She is in her mid-30s. She uses her phone for the weather app to check if she needs a jacket or a hat for her morning run, and for the music app, and for Instagram where she shares her active life and stories about her kids.

Persona #2: Young Local Man

He drives a big truck and has a farmer's tan. He likes going to the hot springs with his buddies or taking a boating trip to Bear Lake. He works at a gas station and lives in his friend's basement when he's not in school. He is in his early 20s. He primarily uses his phone to make TikToks and send Snapchats to his friends from high school. He also uses an investing app. He follows body-builders and Jeeping on Instagram and occasionally gets on Facebook to post about his life so his grandma doesn't get mad.

Scenarios:

  1. Are there any summer festivals happening this month?
  2. How hot will it be on Saturday for the outdoor birthday party?
  3. What are the directions from here to Preston?
  4. What is the location of the nearest hiking trail?
  5. Do I need to cover my plants so they don't freeze tonight?
