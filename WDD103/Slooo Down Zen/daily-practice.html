(vocal)

Set up: Open the mouth, tongue to back of top teeth in an n position.
Practice: Breathe in slowly through the nose. Relax the base of the throat. Breathe out through the nose. Rest in that space. Let the back of your brain float in the quiet dark. Breathe in through the nose. Rest in hang time for a moment. Open the throat and hum a note that is comfortable and low, with the n sound. Keep the note steady. Notice every time it fluxuates. Hum to the end of your breath, but not to discomfort. Repeat this practice several times.
